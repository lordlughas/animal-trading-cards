Blue Whale

The Blue Whale can grow more than 100 feet long. Shocking!, they can also weigh as much as 30 Elephants

  • Scientific Name: Balaenoptera Musculus
  • Average Length: 80-100 feet long
  • Average Lifespan: 80-90 years
  • Habitat: Aquatic

The Blue Whale, is the largest animal ever known to have lived on the planet, including all dinosaurs. Even at birth, it's larger than adults from most other animal species.
